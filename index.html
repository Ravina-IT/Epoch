ABOUT EPOCH


Hello All !!!

Welcome to EPOCH, a National Level Technical Symposium held by IT Association, Kongu Engineering College. And EPOCH’24 is the brand-new edition of this legacy! This event is a combination of fun and frolic, integrated with the technical domain and guaranteeing a lot of enjoyment to participants. This time, the theme of the event is inspired from “Minecraft”, which is a game developed by Microsoft, and now very popular among teenagers.

This is a platform for students from different colleges to showcase their technical prowess and skills, and also making new friends along the way. EPOCH offers a wide range of opportunities in the form of 3 Technical events, 1 workshop, 3 Non-technical events, and an immersive technical workshop! So guys, go out there and enjoy while you can!!
